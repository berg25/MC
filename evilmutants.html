Evil Mutants

The Brotherhood of Mutants is a group dedicated to the cause of mutant superiority over humans. Throughout its various incarnations, the Brotherhood's agenda has gravitated towards either subjugating the human race to the will of mutants or eradicating humanity altogether.

  • Real Name - Evil Mutants

  • First Appearance - X-Men #4 (1964)

  • Origin - X-Men #4 (1964)
