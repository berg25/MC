Red Skull

  • Real Name - Johann Shmidt

  • Height - (original body) 6'1"; (cloned body) 6'2"

  • Weight - (original body) 195 lbs; (cloned body) 240 lbs

  • Abilities - The Red Skull is a fine unarmed combatant, marksman and master of disguise. He possesses a keen mind at military, political, corporate and subversive strategies.

  • Group Affiliations - Special operative in the government of Germany's Nazi Party, Third Reich, former leader of most currently active Nazi Party, Nazi subversives throughout the world, former ally of THEM and A.I.M., former leader of the Nevada-based fragment of Hydra, leader of the People's Militia, former partner of Hate-Monger (Adolf Hitler), Hate-Monger, creator of Scourge of the Underworld

  • First Appearance - Captain America Comics #1 (1941), (modern) Tales of Suspense #66 (1965)

  • Origin - Tales of Suspense #66 (1965), Captain America #298 (1984)

  • Powers - None
