Thanos

The Mad Titan Thanos, a melancholy, brooding individual, consumed with the concept of death, sought out personal power and increased strength, endowing himself with cybernetic implants until he became more powerful than any of his brethren.

  • Real Name - Thanos

  • Height - 6'7

  • Weight - 985lbs

  • Abilities - Master strategist, adept in sciences far beyond Earth technology, some mystical knowledge

  • Group Affiliations - Eternals

  • First Appearance - Iron Man #55 (1973)

  • Origin - Silver Surfer #37 (1990)

  • Powers - Thanos possesses the superhuman physiology of all Eternals, granting him superhuman strength, endurance, reflexes, and agility. His skin in nearly invulnerable, particulary against heat, cold, electricity, radiation, toxins, aging, and disease, and he can survive indefinitely without food or water even before his "curse" from Death left him immortal, unable to die.
