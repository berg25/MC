Ultron

Arguably the greatest and certainly the most horrific creation of scientific genius Dr. Henry Pym, Ultron is a criminally insane rogue sentient robot dedicated to conquest and the extermination of humanity.

  • Real Name - Ultron

  • Height - 6'9

  • Weight - 735lbs

  • Group Affiliations - Phalanx; formerly Sons of Yinsen, Lethal Legion, Masters of Evil

  • First Appearance - Avengers #54 (1968)

  • Origin - Avengers #54 (1968)

  • Powers - Ultron's abilities vary with each redesign, but typically include superhuman strength and durability, the power of flight, and various offensive weapons such as concussion blasters, radiation emitters and his "encephalo-ray", which plunges its victims into a deathlike coma. The latter ray also allows Ultron to mesmerize and outright mind-control his victims, or implant subliminal hypnotic commands within their minds to be enacted at a later time.
