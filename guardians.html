Guardians of The Galaxy

A group of cosmic adventurers led by Star-Lord, the Guardians of the Galaxy protect the universe from threats all across space. The team also includes Drax, Gamora, Groot and Rocket Raccoon!

  • Real Name - Guardians of The Galaxy

  • First Appearance - Marvel Super-Heroes #18 (1969)

  • Origin - Marvel Super-Heroes #18 (1969)
